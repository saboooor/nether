Getting Started

Rules


Please note that the staff have full control over what is allowed and what is not, things may differ.

1. Grief

No griefing anything that a player has built. This includes UNCLAIMED structures.

2. Sensitivity

If a player is uncomfortable about a topic and wants to stop talking about it, you must stop.

3. Slurs

Anything that could possibly be recognized as a slur are all strictly forbidden.

4. Cheating

No x-raying, hacking, or exploiting bugs and glitches. This includes 0-tick farms.

5. Impersonation

Impersonating Staff / acting like you're staff is not permitted.

6. Building

Building inside a 100 block radius outside of a player's build without permission is not allowed.

7. Spam/Caps

No spam or caps, refrain from typing multiple 2-word messages that add up to a sentence.

8. Player Killing

Player killing is NOT allowed if the player has PVP turned off, turn off pvp with /pvp off

9. Advertisements

Advertising anything is forbidden unless it's P2W/Well-known/Related.

10. Behaviour

Keep arguments and toxicity in DMs. Using swears in a malicious matter is considered toxic.

11. Privacy

Leaking IPs or any other info like age, location, phone numbers, etc (Doxxing) is not allowed.

12. Annoyances

If a player is bothered or annoyed by what you're doing that's related to them, you must stop.

Features

AngelChests

When you die, instead of losing your items, they are safely added into a chest at your death location.

ArtMap

Anyone can easily create map arts
with a canvas, execute /artmap for more information.

AuctionHouse

Sell anything for any price with
/ah sell <price> holding the item you want to sell, and someone will buy it!

Claims

Use a Golden Shovel to claim your base and prevent grief, you can also ban people from claims with /claimban

Slime Chunks

To prevent people from cheating, we don't give away the world seed, to find slime chunks, use /slimechunk

DiscordSRV

If you're offline but still want to message the server, you can use the Discord Server: discord.gg/g7hSukX

Bedrock Edition

On the go? No problem! Pull out your phone and join with bedrock.netherdepths.com:27029

Vote Crates

Vote with /vote and get crate keys for crates that give you many different rewards like armor, money, and more!

GSit

Sit, lay or crawl anywhere with /sit, /crawl, and /lay. You can also right click stairs with a free hand to sit!

HorseTPWithMe

You can teleport any ride-able mob with you while riding it, useful if you want to transport villagers with boats.

Marriage

Love someone so much that you want to marry them in the server? (cringe) Marry them with /marry <fiance>!

Silk Spawners

Transport monster spawners and create monster grinders/farms easily with a Silk Touch pickaxe!

Minigames

Play various minigames with your friends: Spleef, Last Man Standing, Elytra Run, Parkour, and more soon!

Ore Money

Earn money while you're mining, but be careful, You may find some pesky creatures while trying to find money!

Loot Crates

Explore the world to find a loot crate with many different rewards like armor, money, bedrock, barriers, and more!

Donation Perks

You can donate to get some perks like Armor Stand Tools, Particles, /stack, Player time and weather, and more!
